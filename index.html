October is...

Breast Cancer Awarness Month

My name is Nichole. I'm a single mom that was diagnosed with grade 3c Breast Cancer in May of 2021 at the age of 33. After 7 surgeries, including a double mastectomy and full hysterectomy, 30 rounds of radiation and just over a year of chemotherapy, October 4th, 2022 will be my last treatment and the beginning of Breast Cancer Awareness Month. I'd like to share some things about Breast Cancer and about my journey in hopes to bring hope and awareness to others! -Nichole
